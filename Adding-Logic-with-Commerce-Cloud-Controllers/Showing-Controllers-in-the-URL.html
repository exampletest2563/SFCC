Showing Controllers in the URL

One thing you might have noticed is that your storefront URL does not reveal which controller is being called.

To enable the display of controllers in the URL, follow these steps:

  1. Go to Merchant Tools > Site Preferences > Storefront URLs.
  2. Uncheck the Enable Storefront URLs checkbox. (In production, this checkbox should always be checked.)
  3. Click Apply.
