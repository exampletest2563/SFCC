Content Assets Menu

To locate Content Assets, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Merchant Tools menu.
  2. Navigate to Content > Content Assets.

Once you click on Content Assets, the Content Assets management page will be displayed.

It's important to note the following detail at the top of the page: Merchant Tools > Content > Libraries > RefArchSharedLibrary > Content.

So, where does this RefArchSharedLibrary come from? It is the name of the metadata file that contains the definitions and data for all content assets in your RefArchGlobal site.
