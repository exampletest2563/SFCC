Grouping Content Assets Using Folders

Now that you understand the importance of Content Assets, letâ€™s explore Folders, an internal structure in Business Manager that is essential for organizing Content Assets.

You can use Business Manager to define or edit a hierarchical folder structure for your content assets. The Library folder page displays the subfolders that exist in your site library, which can be either a private or a shared library.

All folders can contain content assets. You can organize content assets into multiple folders or subfolders as needed. Folders are located close to the Content Assets menu:

Exercise: Create a Folder

  1. In Business Manager, select Site > RefArchGlobal > Merchant Tools > Content > Library Folders.
  2. In the Library Folders section, click the New button to create a new folder.
  3. Provide an ID: salesforceb2c_certification_questions_folder.
  4. Provide a Name: Salesforce B2C Certification Questions Folder.
  5. Set the value Yes for the Online flag.
  6. Click the Apply button to save your folder.
  7. Now go back to Merchant Tools > Content > Library Folders and you should see your folder listed.

Exercise: Assign a Content Asset to a Folder

  1. In Business Manager, select Site > RefArchGlobal > Merchant Tools > Content > Content Assets.
  2. Click on your salesforceb2c_certification_questions_com content asset.
  3. In the Edit page, click on the Folders tab.
  4. Click on the New button.
  5. Check the box for your salesforceb2c_certification_questions_folder folder and click the Assign button.
  6. Now go to Merchant Tools > Content > Library Folders.
  7. Click on your salesforceb2c_certification_questions_folder folder link.
  8. You will see your content asset listed in the Folder Content section.
