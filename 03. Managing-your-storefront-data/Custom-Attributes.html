Custom Attributes

You can create custom attributes to suit the needs of your storefront site. Understanding how to use custom attributes and the responsibilities of maintaining relevant attribute data is crucial for effective site management.

Custom attributes can be added by an Administrator or Developer. To add a custom attribute:

  1. Go to Administration → Site Development → System Object Types → Object → Attribute Definitions tab and click New.
  2. Once the attribute has been added, you may add it to an attribute group.
