Copying a Product

You can copy a product to create variations, such as a deluxe version based on a standard product.

  1. Go to Merchant Tools → Products and Catalogs → Products.
  2. Select the product to copy and click Copy.
  3. Select the copy options to determine the features you want to copy.
  4. Click Finish. Note that the new product ID will have “-1” at the end to make it unique. You can create a new SKU for that product as appropriate.
